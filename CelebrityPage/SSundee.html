SSundee

Ian Stapleton, better known as SSundee, is a YouTuber that uploads modded Minecraft series. His most popular series is his "Lucky Blocks" series, where he opens lucky blocks with his friends and sometimes his wife. He occasionally plays other games, such as "theHunter: Call of the Wild", and other Steam games.

People that he frequently plays Minecraft with are his wife Maddie, Crainer, and Thea (Crainer's fiancée).
